Why We Need to Help Nokia?

The story of an underdog fighting against tech giants

Unmatched Durability

Nokia phones were legendary for their indestructible build quality. The Nokia 3310 could survive drops, water, and even construction work. Modern phones can't match this reliability.

Superior Battery Life

Nokia phones had batteries that lasted for days, even weeks. The 3310 could go up to 2 weeks on a single charge. Today's smartphones struggle to last a full day.

Community & Innovation

Nokia pioneered mobile gaming with Snake, brought cameras to phones, and created the first smartphone. They deserve recognition for shaping mobile technology.

Nokia's Legendary History

From a paper mill to mobile revolution

1865

Foundation

Nokia was founded as a paper mill company in Tampere, Finland. The company name comes from the Nokia River.

1967

Modern Nokia

Nokia Corporation was formed through the merger of Nokia Ab, Finnish Rubber Works, and Finnish Cable Works.

1994

Mobile Revolution

Nokia launched the 2100 series, marking the beginning of their dominance in mobile phones.

2000

Peak Success

The legendary Nokia 3310 was released - the most durable phone ever made, selling over 126 million units.

2007

iPhone Challenge

Apple launched the iPhone, beginning the smartphone revolution that would challenge Nokia's dominance.

2014

Microsoft Era

Nokia's mobile division was acquired by Microsoft, marking the end of an era.

2016

New Beginning

Nokia returned to mobile phones through HMD Global, bringing back the beloved brand.
